In the futuristic Istanbul, there is a hidden shop where people store their memories in digital jars. 
One day, the shopkeeper accidentally opens a forbidden memory that was supposed to be deleted. 
This memory releases a virus that crashes the city's entire artificial intelligence grid and forces people to relearn ancient, forgotten languages. 
A high-stakes chase begins through neon-lit streets and shadows of the past, blending binary codes with old parchments.
The city of Istanbul is controlled by artificial intelligence. This artificial intelligence grid is everywhere.

The shopkeeper hates the artificial intelligence because it deleted the digital jars.
